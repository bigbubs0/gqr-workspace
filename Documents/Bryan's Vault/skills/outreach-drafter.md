# Skill: Outreach Drafter

## Purpose
Draft LinkedIn InMails and emails for candidate outreach.

## Rules
- Always "Hi [Name],"
- Lead with the specific opportunity, not generic intro
- Include one concrete data point (funding, trial phase, team size)
- Keep under 150 words for LinkedIn InMail
- Never use: "excited to connect", "perfect fit", "touch base", "circle back"
- Use "Search Consultant" not "recruiter"
- Use "CV" not "resume"
- Hyphen (-) never em dash
- Apply Sentence Editor rules (impact endings, max economy, kill adverbs)

## Template Structure
1. **Hook** - Why this role/company is worth their attention (1 sentence with data)
2. **Role** - Title, company, what makes it unique (2-3 sentences)
3. **Ask** - Clear CTA: "Would you be open to a 15-minute call this week?"

## Output
Save drafts to `/recruiting/outreach/YYYY-MM-DD-candidatename-company.md`

All drafts go to `/review/` for Bryan's approval before sending.
